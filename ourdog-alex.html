HELLO, I AM ALEX

I came into SCARS care with my siblings. We are named after Leverage characters as my foster mom says we will steal anyone's heart. I like to sit back and watch my siblings to see what I can learn before diving in. That's how I found the best hiding spot on a shelf in the closet. My foster mom says I make the silliest faces when I am enjoying my scratches and cuddles but I am still learning how to cuddly "nicely". Apparently my foster mom doesn't appreciate me using her hair as a pull toy and her ankles are not chew toys. I use my pee pads most of the time. I like being around my foster siblings and exploring new parts of the house. If I sound like the right pup for you just wait until you meet me and I will steal your heart.
