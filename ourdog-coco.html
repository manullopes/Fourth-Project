HELLO, I AM COCO

My name is Coco. I came into care with my mom but I am on my own now. I need help making new friends and with socialization. I am a very nervous girl who will require some training a LOTS of playdates. Slow introductions are always best, and I will slowly gain confidence around another dog. I like children, playing fetch and tug of war. I am working on being house trained. I am comfortable sleeping in my crate at night or for some quiet time during the day. I would be a great addition to a home with kids, I love kids! I am also looking for a family that will be very patient with me in regards to socializing me, especially if they have a resident pup at home. It may take me a few days to trust him.
